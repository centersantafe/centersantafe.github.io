# Calls for Entry

CENTER, a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit organization based in Santa Fe, New Mexico, has provided direct funding and project advancement packages for photographic and lens-based works since 1994.

## The 2026 Calls for Entry Are Now Closed

Thank you to everyone who applied to the 2026 CENTER Calls for Entry, which closed on February 28, 2026. We are grateful to the photographers and visual storytellers who shared their work with us this year.

Submissions are currently under review, and applicants will be notified once the jury process is complete. We appreciate your interest in CENTER and the vibrant creative community that continues to grow around this program.

Please check back in May for the winners, and toward the end of the year for updates on the 2027 Calls for Entry.

## General Eligibility & Information

We accept submissions from photographers and lens-based artists located around the world for all available categories. Work derived from all photographic processes is accepted, as well as video and mixed media that is photo-based.
