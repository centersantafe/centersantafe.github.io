# CENTER History

From scholarship funding in 1993 to a full home for the photographic arts today, CENTER has continually grown its programs and partnerships.

### 2025
- CENTER hosts the Grand Opening for its new exhibition space on May 30, 2025, with the exhibition _From the Field: Nuclear Legacy_
- CENTER worked with the International Board of Credentialing and Continuing Education Standards (IBCCES) to be designated as a Certified Autism Center™ (CAC)
- CENTER opens a new exhibition and community space in Santa Fe, New Mexico

### 2024
- CENTER celebrates its 30th Year Anniversary
- The annual Project Presentations return to in-person programming
- CENTER hosts an Anniversary Print Sale featuring 30 alum
- CENTER receives a significant grant from the National Endowment for the Humanities to support the formalization of our digital archive — _Activating the Archive: Preserving 21st-Century Photography_

### 2023
- Taking on the next chapter of the Blue Earth Alliance fiscal sponsorships programming with CENTER's new program – Blue Earth Fiscal Sponsorship
- Review Santa Fe returns to form in Santa Fe, New Mexico
- Expanded Project Labs: Seminars and Workshops
- Inaugural Jay and Susie Tyrrell Excellence in Works by Hand Award
- Revamped Member benefits – Access to CENTER's Circle: Special Content & Resources and Connections Network

### 2022
- New Member Benefits: CENTER's Circle: Special Content & Resources
- Review Santa Fe returns to Santa Fe, New Mexico
- New Program: _The Democratic Lens: Photography & Civic Engagement_ discussion series examining how images have shaped America's collective memory and inspired civic participation
- Circular Solutions 2021 & 2021 Award and Grant Winners Exhibition at the Turchin Center for the Visual Arts

### 2021
- Inaugural Awards highlighting specific themes: Personal, Social, and Environmental
- Me&EVE Award evolves to the Me&Eve Grant
- First ever online portfolio reviews for Review Santa Fe

### 2020
- First year without Review Santa Fe
- New Program: _Photography 20/20 Compendium_ investigates the past 20 years of photography in interviews, panel discussions, critical essays, and photographer presentations
- Inaugural Project Labs: Seminars & Mentorship
- The Excellence in Teaching Award evolves to the Callanan Excellence in Teaching Award, after CENTER's founder Reid Callanan

### 2019
- Inaugural me&EVE Award supporting women, supported by alum Dorie Hagler
- Inaugural Excellence in Multimedia Award
- Mayor pro-tem of Santa Fe attends Review Santa Fe Opening Night Lecture
- Lecture _A Box of Ten Photographs: Odyssey of Diane Arbus_ by Smithsonian Curator John Jacob
- Outdoor installation _Climate Truth in the Anthropocene_
- Free Film: USA attends Review Santa Fe

### 2018
- Inaugural Photographic Book Fair
- New Producer's Choice Award
- First solo exhibition for an artist – Joel-Peter Witkin
- PhotoSummer Exhibitions _Conjured Futures_ and _Color Cue: Explorations in Perception_

### 2017
- _Art & Oppression_ Exhibition, Panel Discussion, workshop, and reading room at the Marion Center, Santa Fe University of Art and Design
- _Zenith_ Exhibition at the Marion Center
- _Global Perspectives: International Photographic Award Winners_ Exhibition at the Marion Center

### 2016
- Receives OTAB Grant and Collaborative Arts Marketing Grant for the City of Santa Fe
- PhotoSummer with 4 CENTER Exhibitions
- Review Santa Fe offers Festival Passes

### 2015
- PhotoSummer initiated
- First honoree dinner with Anne Wilkes Tucker

### 2014
- Exhibition at the Marion Center for Photographic Arts

### 2013
- Exhibition at the CCA
- Produces Artist Labs with 4 artist residents on the IHM Campus and Santa Fe University of Art and Design

### 2012
- Receives first National Endowment for the Arts Grant and Santa Fe Arts Commission Grant

### 2011
- Exhibition at the NM Museum of Art
- Creative Edge Conference evolves to the Portfolio Bootcamp
- Inaugural Project Development Grant

### 2009
- Exhibition of Award Winners of the Photographic Center Northwest

### 2008
- Inaugural Review LA

### 2007
- CENTER offers a Membership program

### 2006
- Santa Fe Center for Photography Photoplus Expo Portfolio Reviews
- Name change to CENTER official in December with new branding and identity package

### 2005
- _Exploring the Creative Edge: A Retreat Style Conference_

### 2003
- New name — Santa Fe Center for Photography
- Inaugural Santa Fe Prize for Photography
- Review Santa Fe becomes the only juried event of its kind in the country

### 2001
- Inaugural Review Santa Fe

### 1998
- Inaugural Excellence in Photographic Teaching Awards
- First annual Project Symposium produced by the Center for Photographic Projects under the aegis of SFCVA

### 1997
- _Assignment Earth_ — single image awards initiated

### 1995
- Inaugural Project Competition
- Santa Fe Center for Visual Arts receives 501(c)(3) incorporation

### 1994
- First Project Workshop initiated by Sam Abell — "one of my motives was that it would be a workshop that did not end"

### 1993
- Santa Fe Center for Visual Arts (SFCVA), CENTER's original name, was created for the initial purpose of funding scholarships
