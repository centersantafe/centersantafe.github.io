# Portfolio Reviewers

Each year, CENTER carefully selects curators, editors, book publishers, gallery and festival directors, and others to attend Review Santa Fe. Our reviewers offer feedback and opportunities for project advancement, contributing to the event's atmosphere of community and support.

Approximately 45 portfolio reviewers will be in attendance. The 2026 reviewers confirmed so far are listed below. Check back as confirmations come in on a rolling basis.
