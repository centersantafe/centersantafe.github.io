## Eligibility

CENTER accepts submissions from photographers and lens-based artists around the world. Work can have been made at any time, and work that has been previously submitted may be re-submitted as we invite new jurors each year. Applicants may submit multiple projects within the same category, as well as the same project to multiple categories.

Work derived from all photographic processes is accepted, and mixed media that is photo-based. Only Review Santa Fe and Blue Earth Fiscal Sponsorship applications accept video or multimedia files for submission.

**Who cannot enter** — CENTER employees, board members, advisory council members, contest sponsors, and family members of the aforementioned may not apply to the Awards or Grants. Previous CENTER cash award/grant winners may enter but are ineligible to win again for up to 3 years. Photographers represented by a major international photographic agency are not eligible to apply to the Project Development Grant and Project Launch Grant.

**Me&Eve Award** — Accepts submissions from women and/or LGBTQIA+ identifying photographers who are 40 years of age and over. This includes female-identified, non-binary, gay, lesbian, bisexual, transgender, queer, gender non-conforming, questioning, asexual, intersex, and two-spirited identifying individuals.

**Grant funds** — Granted funds may be used for most expenses related to the project. Please note that no additional funds are available for exhibition or travel expenses to Santa Fe; however, you may use the allotted funds for those expenses. The cash award is the total amount dispersed.

**Use of AI** — By submitting images, the artist represents that the images are the artist's own work. Submissions may not include AI-generated imagery; digital post-processing is permitted.

If you have any questions, please email [programs@centersantafe.org](mailto:programs@centersantafe.org) before submitting your application.

## File Requirements

**What types of files are accepted?**
We accept JPG, PDF, and PNG files for all application categories. Additional accepted file types for only the Multimedia Award, Review Santa Fe, and Blue Earth Fiscal Sponsorship include MP3, MP4, and MOV. Video files can be hosted on Vimeo, YouTube, or a similar service, or uploaded directly in the submission form. Note that the larger the files are, the longer they take to upload for the jurors.

**What are the file & sizing requirements?**
JPGs and PNGs should be sized at 1400 pixels maximum on the longest side with a 150 maximum dpi. PNG or PDF documents should be under 5 MB. Video and audio files should be under 3 minutes and limited to 200 MB each.

**How should I label my files?**
Begin the filename with a lowercase alphabetical letter, an underscore, and the image title — for example, `a_Title.jpg`, `b_Title.jpg`.

**Do you accept or require a résumé or CV for application?**
No. Résumés are requested only from Blue Earth Sponsorship applicants.

**Do you accept artwork generated by AI?**
No. Submissions may not include AI-generated imagery, although digital post-processing is permitted.

## Application FAQs

**I just paid — where do I apply / upload my files?**
You will upload and submit your work within our online submission portal that is sent to you via email once your payment has been processed.

**Where is my confirmation email?**
Please check your spam/junk folder for an email labeled "Awards, Grants, or Review Santa Fe Payment Confirmation." If you don't receive it after 30 minutes, please email [programs@visitcenter.org](mailto:programs@visitcenter.org). Please add our emails to your contacts so our communications do not end up in your spam folder — payment receipts come from `programs@visitcenter.org`.

**Why can I not access Submittable?**
You most likely do not have a CENTER Submittable account. A standard Submittable login will not work on our secondary site. Note that submitting payment in the CENTER entry form does not create a CENTER Submittable account. The information requested at the time of payment and submitting your work is separate.

**I just signed up as a Member — where is my submission login information?**
Becoming a CENTER Member does not count as payment to the Calls for Entry. You must fill out the application form and pay to receive access to upload images.

**May I submit two or more bodies of work to the same category?**
Yes — you can submit more than one portfolio to the same category, but you must pay for each additional portfolio. This applies to all submission categories.

**Can I submit more than 6 images to the Awards?**
Yes. After you have submitted, email [programs@visitcenter.org](mailto:programs@visitcenter.org) with the number of images, and we can send an invoice through PayPal for the additional files.

**Can I upload the same portfolio to multiple categories?**
Absolutely. You can submit the same portfolio to any number of categories.

**Can I edit my submission?**
Once you have submitted your application, you can submit a request to edit through the submission portal in your drafted submission, or email us and request we re-open your submission for editing. Changes are allowed until the final submission deadline.

## Criteria and Jury Process

**What are the selection criteria?**
1. Projects, as well as all submitted text, should be examples of excellence within the discipline, both visually and conceptually.
2. The award, grant, and Review Santa Fe recipients should be at a place in their work and career where they can benefit from the exposure, networks, and opportunities that CENTER offers.
3. The photographer must be committed and work to advance the project with the support received.

**Do you "pre-screen"?**
No. All work submitted is seen by the designated category jurors.

**What are jurors looking for?**
Cohesive, compelling bodies of work — or singular images — that are ready for international recognition and support.

**Is there a time limit within which the photographs should have been taken?**
No. You may submit work regardless of the date it was created.

**Can we apply as a duo or group?**
Yes. Use one artist's name and information on the application, and in your project statement, note that this is a collaboration. To ensure a blind jury process, please do not list the group of artists' names in the project statement. Note that winners will receive one slot at Review Santa Fe, so the group would sit together in front of each reviewer.

**Will I receive feedback on the work?**
Due to the number of submissions, the jurors cannot provide specific feedback at this time.

## Review Santa Fe Symposium FAQs

**Where does Review Santa Fe take place?**
The Review Santa Fe Photo Symposium and portfolio reviews take place in Fall 2026 at the LaFonda on the Plaza hotel, Santa Fe, NM. The official date will be announced soon.

**How many reviews will I receive?**
Tuition includes up to nine 20-minute formal reviews.

**How long are the portfolio reviews?**
Twenty minutes each.

**Will I have a choice of reviewers?**
After you have been invited, you will have the opportunity to rank the [reviewers](/review-santa-fe/reviewers) in order of your preference. You will then enter your rankings in an electronic lottery system approximately two weeks prior to the event. Over 80% of the participants receive reviews with reviewers ranked among their top 15 choices.

**Can I show more than one portfolio during my review sessions?**
Yes — you can bring more than one series during the portfolio review sessions.

## Blue Earth Fiscal Sponsorship

**Important considerations before applying:**

- Before sending in your proposal, please make sure you understand that the CENTER 501(c)(3) Fiscal Sponsorship is a U.S. tax status that may be used to approach companies, foundations, and private individuals for tax-deductible donations.
- This Fiscal Sponsorship does not include direct project funding or grants. Photographers and filmmakers are solely responsible for fundraising for their project. Receiving the Fiscal Sponsorship does not eliminate the work required on your part to secure grants and donations to fund the project.
- All requests for funds under the organization's name are subject to CENTER approval and are made on behalf of CENTER, which will administer the funds and — after deducting a 10% administration fee — distribute those funds to the project as it progresses.
- There are only 1–3 spots available per year. Not all work that is submitted will be accepted.
