# Portfolio Walk

CENTER's annual Portfolio Walk provides a platform for the public to view the [Review Santa Fe 100](http://reviewsantafelisting.org/) photographic projects. For one night only on Saturday, November 1, CENTER welcomes gallerists, curators, editors, and other photography enthusiasts to view a broad range of contemporary and documentary projects exploring some of our most urgent social, environmental, and political issues.

Hundreds of lens-based artists from around the world apply to take part in the weekend's Review Santa Fe Photo Symposium. The participants are selected by an independent jury of industry professionals. The public is invited to join us for an extraordinary opportunity to view and take home compelling artwork and books created by photographic artists from around the globe.
